Take Nothing but Pictures

Jul 22nd, 2012 | Comments

Posted in Nature of Things

In my youth I remember visiting many National Parks found throughout the Northwestern United States. At the start of many trails would be a sign that read:

“Take nothing but pictures, leave nothing but photographs” Photograph Credit: JC Olivera. Currently used without permission.

I’m no John Muir but this phrase certainly defines my place in this world.
