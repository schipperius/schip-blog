Oh, Hello There!

Jun 10th, 2012

In late July 2008, I had no IT skills what-so-ever, I had written a book a few years earlier and wanted to put it up on the Web. Easy, or so I thought. I bought Elizabeth Castro’s book, HTML, XHTML, and CSS and began learning web design.

In August 2010, I jumped into the Ruby on Rails environment, and now almost four years into the the World Wide Web, I thought I would write (and share) some of my learning experiences.

Does the Internet need another blog on web development? I don’t think so, but I do think I am able to provide a certain perspective that currently seems to be missing: the non-IT entry into web design and development.

Hopefully these articles will help others, in some way, as so many writers continue to help me.
